Consulting & Digital Strategy

Digital strategy is the key to a successful e-commerce in China. We will help you leverage your brand equity and product offering to fit the digital environment in China. An appropriate digital strategy allows you to precisely understand your digital market, your competitors, the best practices for a successful business, especially under the digital landscape in China. The uniqueness of the Chinese ecommerce ecosystem compared to the western world requires a unique market entry startegy to set a right start. We will give you the keys to define the most optimal approach of entering the Chinese digital world and your online brand / channel positioning.

Consulting & Digital eCommerce stragegy in China

Marketplace

Digital Marketplaces in China are the main channels for brands and e-merchants to drive orders. Tmall/Taobao and JD count already for more the 65% of the total eCommerce market in China. It is mandatory for a brand in China to understand why and how they should use marketplaces for their eCommerce. We will help you to understand the Chinese marketplaces, to set-up your shop on Tmall/Taobao/JD or other vertical marketplaces. We will operate your marketplace shop to drive traffic and sales by optimizing your online advertising budget on these marketplaces.

Magento or Drupal? Choosing the right platform for your e-commerce website is a critical decision for your e-business. Magento and Drupal are different eCommerce technologies that fit different needs and different budget ranges. We will advise and help you find the right technology for your eCommerce website. As long term partner with the best eCommerce techologies, we offer state-of-the-art solutions to support your e-shop. With over 100 eCommerce projects completed in the past 10 years, we can proudly guarantee that our technology is secure, reliable, and scalable for your e-business.

Digital Marketing & Social Media

Your successful e-business cannot be achieved without Digital Marketing. The digital landscape in China is completely different from the rest of the world. With leaders like Baidu, Weibo, Taobao/Tmall, you will need a specific digital strategy for China - Search Engine Optimization (SEO) and Pay-per-click (PPC) with Baidu & 360, Social Networks with Weibo and WeChat, Direct Marketing & eCRM. We will advise you, on how to build &and run the most efficient and profitable digital marketing in China in China and how to allocate your budget between each digital marketing tool. We will work as your business partner to optimize your revenue.
